    Indian Food is healthy?
    Indian food can be healthy because it's made from fresh ingredients, uses spices with medicinal properties, and is less processed than Western food. Indian food is known for its variety of dishes, which can include vegetables, legumes, and spices. Some of the healthiest foods in Indian cuisine include: Lentils, Millet, Tomatoes, Ginger, Turmeric, Cinnamon, Sweet potato, and Spinach
